I am a PhD candidate at the MSCS Department of the University of Illinois Chicago. I am fortunate to be advised by Lev Reyzin. I am part of the Mathematical Computer Science group, and my research focuses on using combinatorics and probability theory to study theoretical computer science problems. Recently, I also started to learn about and work on problems in deep learning. I am currently on the job market.

Before UIC, I was an undergraduate student at the University of Florida, where I majored in math and did a minor in violin performance. And before that, I attended the United World College, USA in Montezuma, NM.
