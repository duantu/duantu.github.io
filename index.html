I am a PhD candidate at the MSCS Department of the University of Illinois Chicago. I am fortunate to be advised by Lev Reyzin. My area of focus is Mathematical Computer Science, and I am particularly interested in machine learning theory, combinatorial optimization, and applications of probability theory in theoretical computer science.
